Connecting With Other Artists

Instagram, Tumblr and Facebook

Artists try to cater towards picture sharing websites and social media. Instagram is popular for artists due to its simplicity in sharing photos, though it's difficult to communicate with your followers. Tumblr holds more active accounts, but Tumlbr and Facebook are both capable of posting without a photo and are easy to post with a photo while placed in the layout nicely.

Better Your Artwork

The only step to improve your art is to practice. Use reference images, carry a sketchbook and a pencil to help with this. Art classes and informative videos help but will not completely change your level of art. The most important tool you have is time. Use it to practice.
